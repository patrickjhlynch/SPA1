Seeing Networks in Wellington

Over two days I searched the streets of Wellington City, trying to find traces of the internet - be it markings on the footpath showing where underground cables were running, or all-too conspicuous cameras in front of shop windows. I took photographs and noted where the most devices were gathered in one location.

This website is the result of my efforts to document the network devices found in Wellington City.
